What is BuildControl?

BuildControl - it is an intelligent delivery management system, providing information and control of materials on complex construction sites in real time.

Track delivery throughout the supply chain

From supplier to subcontractor, you always know where your materials that make ordinary waste a thing of the past and reduce accidents on site.

Optimize and control your onsite arrival

Delivery vehicles arrive at fixed intervals to the pre-assigned bays where the unloading equipment is ready and waiting for them.

Take control of real-time data

Intelligent reporting empowers you to make decisions in depending on how things are at the moment. No more manual concrete pouring reports or a monthly check of 10.000 invoice invoices, all available online without any effort.

Delivery just got easier

We will take care of all your various deliveries and their many specialized unloading requirements.

Concrete

Concrete deliveries are precisely planned, and its condition is monitored, that provides the maximum possible installation time.

Recycling

Waste is monitored and sorted, and the fullness of containers digitally tracked.

Timbering

All timbering is monitored from arrival to departure, and its condition controlled to minimize loss and damage.

Prefabricated panels

Prefabricated panels arrive in turn, their storage on site is monitored and their installation is planned and monitored.

Facades

Facade elements are delivered sequentially, their assembly is supervised, and movement is tracked.

Finishing

Materials arrive on time, are marked and tracked in detail for installation.

Scaffolding

Scaffolding is tracked from receipt to storage, use and return, so it is always clear what is in place and where.

Equipment

Equipment and operators are guaranteed to be available when needed, and their working status is monitored.

Electrical and Plumbing

Delivery is carried out in exact sequence for complex installation, which reduces or eliminates the need for storage.
